Cube Dancer

A 3D Platformer Game

Cube Dancer is a Platformer game developed using Unity3D and C#. Navigate the obstacle courses and slide your way to the finish. Unlock mechanics as you play and combine them to clear tricky areas. Sometimes you have to think outside of the cube.





Cube Dancer was my first game developed using Unity. It was originally developed for PC, then ported to android with improved controls.
